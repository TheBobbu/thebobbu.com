One Almighty Druid

A fine gentleman named Mirko Gallotta sent me a message a little while ago, enquiring about whether or not I could ship Edwina’s Headband to Italy. Of course, I did, and he was ever so kind enough to send me some photos of the marvellous way they were put to use!

Mirko’s druid costume was for Lucca’s Comics and Games festival, a jolly fine looking annual fair about the world of manga, anime, comics, video games, board games and role-playing games. Makes me proud that my creations have now visited more countries than I have myself!

P.S. You may have noticed that I have changed the “Commission Something” button on the page into a “Contact Me” button. That’s because I am no longer taking on commissions. I’ve got a day job now, you see, and it’s taking up a lot of my time. This means that while you will be seeing less frequent updates, I hope that what I post is going to be even more splendid! And much, much more fun for me to create, which is – after all – why I do all this creative larking about.
