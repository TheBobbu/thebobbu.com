Clockwork Jaw Earrings

The Steam Rodents of Outer Hyspania are well known for their powerful jaws. So powerful are they, in fact, that they are known to emit a powerful force even after the rodent has died. This force can be harnessed as a power source or used unadulterated to make the wearer irresistibly attractive. Or so the local witch-doctors say.
